What is a combi drill?

A combi drill is an all purpose drill that can perform a variety of tasks and can also be referred to as a hammer drill driver or an impact drill and is usually the most expensive of these 3 options. The majority of combi drills will have a hammer setting which this gives the drill a similar impact force as well as a rotational force. This allows you to drill into concrete and masonry. If the combi drill is being used on masonry work, ensure you use specific masonry drill bits with a diamond or carbide tip and use a slower speed setting. The slower speed settings should also be used on materials that require high high torque. Higher drill speeds should be used when drilling into wood or metal.
